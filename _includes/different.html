What Makes Us Different

HackHer413’s mission to increase diversity in the tech industry will be present throughout our entire event. Our goal manifests into three categories: empowering, connecting, and inspiring students. From start to finish, here’s how HackHer413 will embody these goals.

Empower

Intimidated to attend your first hackathon? Don’t worry! HackHer413 will be geared to any level of coder and innovator and strongly encourage newcomers to attend. We will have beginner-to-advanced workshops, regular check ins with hackers on their experience, and motivating events to keep participants’ spirits high!

Connect

Worried about meeting people at the event? HackHer413 will have teambuilding, networking, and friend-making activities throughout the event. Participants will be surrounded by students and professionals from tons of different backgrounds, with the same curiosity and passion for tech.

Inspire

Wondering if computer science is for you? At HackHer413, we are committed to inspiring women and non-binary students to explore the endless possibilities in computer science and technology. From industry panels to inspirational speakers, it is our job at HackHer413 to leave participants feeling like they call themselves hack(h)ers.
